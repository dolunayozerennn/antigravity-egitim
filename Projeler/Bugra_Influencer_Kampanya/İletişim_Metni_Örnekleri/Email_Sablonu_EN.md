# 📧 English Email Template — Event Collaboration

## Subject:
```
Collaboration opportunity — {Name}
```

## Body:
```
Hi {Name},

I've been following your content on {Platform} and your posts about {niche} really caught my attention.

I'm reaching out regarding {Event Name} — we'd love to explore a collaboration with you.
[Brief info about the event — when, where, who it's for]

We believe working together would help us reach exactly the right audience.

Would you be open to a quick call to discuss the details?

Best regards,
{Sender Name}
{Contact Info}
```

---

## 💡 Personalization Tips

- `{Name}` → Influencer's name or username (without @)
- `{Platform}` → Instagram / TikTok
- `{niche}` → Influencer's content area (music, lifestyle, entertainment, etc.)
- `{Event Name}` → Your event's name
- Slightly tweak the message for each influencer — identical messages may look like spam
